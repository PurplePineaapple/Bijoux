# Bijoux
 Bijoux is a mod that adds to and enhances the overall experience of mining by adding a variety of jewels and gems. From Ruby to Opal to Platinum, Bijoux has a variety of gems that serve various puroses. For list of features, items, and screenshots see the [wiki](https://github.com/PurplePineaapple/Bijoux/wiki).
